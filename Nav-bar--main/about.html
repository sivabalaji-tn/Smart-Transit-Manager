Our Story

Our journey began with a simple idea: making school transportation safe, reliable, and transparent. We realized parents and students often worry about bus timings and safety. With the latest GPS and web technologies, our Live Bus Tracking System provides real-time updates, route info, and alerts directly to your phone or computer.

Today, we proudly serve multiple schools and institutions, improving transportation efficiency and student safety every day.
